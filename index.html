Hotel The Great Ganga, a tranquil and intimate retreat at the bank of Ganges

Hotel The Great Ganga is located in and around one of the oldest and prominent areas of Rishikesh, known as Muni-ki-reti, where holy Ganges descends from majestic Himalayas and enters the plains of northern India. Our hotel is designed in such a manner that guests can enjoy the enchanting panoramic view of the holy Ganges, surrounded by the lush green serene hills. It takes only 5 minutes of casual walk to reach the banks of the holy Ganges from the hotel.

Travelers can stay, experience and explore the city of Rishikesh for yoga, meditation, mountain trekking, adventure and observe animals in their natural habitat.
